PvP Arena Stadium

By EpiclyEpic321

This is a cool little PvP Arena that has seats to make it a stand. There is one lever that controls all 12 pistons (3 pistons in each cage and 4 cages.) Getting to the middle is hard and same with going back to the sides from the middle. Redstone might need to be reconfigured just by simply remiving a redstone then putting it back.
